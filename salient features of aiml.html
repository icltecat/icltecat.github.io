Salient features of Department of CSE (AI&ML)

• The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (AI&ML) was established in the year 2020 with an intake of 60. The intake is gradually increased to 132 by the year 2021..

• GIST offers a four-year under-graduate B.Tech course in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning which aims to develop a strong foundation.

•This course is best suited for students seeking to build world-class expertise in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and emerging technologies which help to stand in the crowd and grow careers in the upcoming technological era.
